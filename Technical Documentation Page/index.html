About Us

We are gamers who love playing RPG games and fantasy. We started on console games and a little PC gaming but never had the chance to get into the larger TTRPG style games. Every time we looked around for a local store to go and get new games or accessories, we could never find one that catered more towards table-top games. So we decided to change that and open one ourselves.

Firstly, we wanted to create a space where people could not only come get table-top games, but also the fantasy card games, as well as accessories and collectables. Secondly, we wanted a place where gamers could meet up and have a space to play those games as well as meet like minded people who are into the same kind of gaming.

Memberships

We will be offering monthly and yearly memberships as well as day passes. Included in the memberships will be access to use of our game room, free paint with purchase of a mini as well as space to paint. You will also recieve 1 guest day pass per month.

We will also have a survey for first time customers to fill-out. These surveys will help us make sure our customers and members are enjoying their time with us. First time customers filling out the survey will also get 5% off their first purchase.

Family Game Night

Looking for a night out with the family? We have family game night packs available. These game night packs will include access to our game room for a block of time, as well as access to our library of table-top and board games.

We may also offer in the future monthy memberships for families.
